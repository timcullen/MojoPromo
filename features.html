Features

Security

MoneyMojo is protected by state of the art security. All your communications with Mojo servers are encrypted using the latest 2MB encryption keys. Look out for padlock and green background in your address bar. This feature provided by our security partner Thawte Inc gives you confidence that you are logged into the authentic MoneyMojo site.

Stay up-to-date

Track spending against budgets

Create a budget for your future spending (or allow MoneyMojo to do it for you based on your historic spending!) and track how you are doing against budget. Look like you might overspend? Might be time to cut back on a few luxuries! Things looking good? Celebrate with that night out you've been promising yourself.

Scheduled Transactions

Enter regular transactions you already know about (for example your direct debits and standing orders) just once, and allow MoneyMojo to record them at the right times every month (or week, or year, or however often you like).

Forecasting

Let MoneyMojo show you where you'll be next week, next month or next year. MoneyMojo takes your current balances, and combines them with your scheduled transactions and your budget to forecast your future cashflow.

Manage Investments

MoneyMojo lets you track your investments. You can record purchases and sales of shares of course. But also record other transactions, update share prices online and track performance of your portfolio over time and against standard benchmarks. You can even forecast what your portfolio will look like when you retire (based on easily changed assumptions)

Import from Microsoft Money

For users of Microsoft Money, MoneyMojo will import all of your data automatically. Accounts, categories, transactions, budgets, scheduled transactions and investments. All available to you without having to re-enter anything.

Insightful Reports and Charts

MoneyMojo provides you with insight, not just information, about your financial position.

Automatic Upgrades and Backup

MoneyMojo software runs on our superfast servers in the Cloud. When we upgrade the software (which we do all the time to fix those annoying little bugs and share exciting new features!) you get it instantly. No more waiting around for new upgrades to be downloaded to your desktop. You always know you're working with the latest version of the software. In addition, the Cloud lets us take charge of your backup centrally. No more lost data. No more "Doh, where did I put that backup?"
